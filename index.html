📄 Privacy Policy — Lotus Habits

Effective Date: April 20, 2026
Last Updated: April 20, 2026

1. Overview

Lotus Habits (“the App”, “we”, “our”) is a privacy-focused habit tracking and productivity application built for Android.

  • The App does not operate any servers or backend infrastructure
  • The App does not collect or store personal data on our systems
  • All data is stored locally on your device only

However, the App uses limited third-party services (such as advertising providers) that may independently collect certain information.

2. Data Storage (On-Device Only)

All data is stored exclusively on your device.

  • Habits and schedules
  • Habit completion records
  • Daily tracker entries
  • Tasks and to-do items
  • Pomodoro session data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Notification configurations
  • App lock (biometric preference only)

Storage methods:

  • SQLite
  • SharedPreferences

✔ This data is not transmitted, accessible, or stored externally.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No user accounts
  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No cross-app tracking
  • No remote storage

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob.

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device info

This data is handled by Google, not us.

User Control: You can disable ad personalization in Android settings.

4.2 In-App Purchases

  • Processed by Google Play Store
  • No payment data stored by us

5. App Permissions

  • Biometric — App lock
  • Notifications — Reminders
  • Exact Alarm — Scheduling
  • Storage — Backup/restore

6. Data Backup & Export

  • Manual backups only
  • Stored where you choose
  • We do not access backup files

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Data stays on device
  • Uninstalling deletes app data
  • Backup files must be deleted manually

8. Data Security

  • Use device lock
  • Enable app lock
  • Keep device updated

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data.

10. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date.
