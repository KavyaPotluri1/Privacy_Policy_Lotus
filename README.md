📄 Privacy Policy — Lotus Habits

**Effective Date:** April 20, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Lotus Habits (“the App”, “we”, “our”) is a privacy-focused habit tracking and productivity application built for Android.

* The App **does not operate any servers or backend infrastructure**
* The App **does not collect or store personal data on our systems**
* All data is stored **locally on your device only**

However, the App uses limited third-party services (such as advertising providers) that may independently collect certain information, as described below.

---

## 2. Data Storage (On-Device Only)

All data created and used within the App is stored exclusively on your device.

### Data stored locally includes:

* Habits and schedules
* Habit completion records (including full and partial completions)
* Daily tracker entries (e.g., productive, tired, busy, etc.)
* Tasks and to-do items
* Pomodoro session data
* App settings and preferences
* Notification configurations
* App lock (biometric preference only)

### Storage methods:

* SQLite (local database)
* SharedPreferences (local configuration storage)

✔ This data:

* Is not transmitted to us
* Is not accessible by us
* Is not stored on any external server

---

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not:

* Create user accounts
* Collect personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
* Track your activity across apps or services
* Store any user data on remote servers

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Although the App itself operates offline, it uses third-party services that may collect data independently.

---

### 4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

The free version of the App displays ads using Google AdMob.

Google may automatically collect certain information, including:

* Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
* IP address (used to estimate general location)
* Ad interaction data (e.g., ad views, taps)
* Device and system information

This data is:

* Collected directly by Google
* Processed under Google’s own privacy policy
* Not accessible to us

#### User Controls

You can manage ad personalization in your Android device settings:

* “Opt out of Ads Personalization”

---

### 4.2 In-App Purchases (Remove Ads)

If you purchase the “Remove Ads” upgrade:

* Payments are processed by Google Play Store
* We do not process or store payment information
* We do not receive any financial details

---

## 5. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions, used only for on-device functionality:

| Permission              | Purpose                                |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Biometric (Fingerprint) | Optional app lock                      |
| Notifications           | Habit reminders                        |
| Exact Alarm (Android)   | Accurate scheduling                    |
| Storage / Files         | Backup & restore (user-initiated only) |

✔ No permission is used for tracking or external data collection.

---

## 6. Data Backup & Export

The App provides an optional manual backup feature.

* Backup files are saved to a location you choose
* We do not access, upload, or store backup files
* You are responsible for managing and securing backup files

If you share backup files (e.g., via cloud storage or messaging), the data may be accessible to others.

---

## 7. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally:

* Data remains on your device until you delete it
* To delete all data:

  * Uninstall the App

Backup files must be deleted manually from your storage if created.

---

## 8. Data Security

Your data security depends on your device’s protections.

We recommend:

* Using a device lock (PIN, password, or biometric)
* Enabling the App Lock feature
* Keeping your device software up to date

Because the App does not transmit data, exposure risks are minimized.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under 13.

* We do not knowingly collect personal data from children
* All data remains stored locally on the device

Parents or guardians can:

* Review data directly on the device
* Delete all data by uninstalling the App

* Changes will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date
* Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy
